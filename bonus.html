GOODY LIEUTENANT

      Goody,the Lieutenant is a principled,disciplined young man with a strong sense of justice.Since his childhood days Goody dreamed of becoming a Military officer and give his best to serve the Nation.Goody was raised in a Middle class family whose parents are farmers.At first Goody's parents opposed his decision of joining Military but his persistence made them support him contently.He joined Army as a soldier and was promoted to Lieutenant for his exceptional service.

Physical/Personality traits of Goody:

  1. Goody is Sincere,generous and kind.
  2. Goody is shy and reserved.
  3. Goody has gorgeous black,naturally straight hair.
  4. Goody has Doe eyes and Bunny smile.
  5. Goody appears to be emotionless but he is quite sensitive.
  6. Goody tattooed his Birth Flower on his forearm.
